The Play Bud(s) Story

Play Buds offers a small selection of cannabis-themed games, and asks players to self-assess their cannabis-intoxication level as they accumulate points for ranking and redemption. Why do we ask how high you are? Play Buds would like to see how game choice and play vary at different levels of intoxication in an effort to assist researchers in associating functional impairment levels with cannabis intake. Thanks for playing!
